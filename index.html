Directions & Info

The wedding starts at 4 pm, but you're welcome to arrive from 3:30 and enjoy a drink!
Park on Victoria road, and follow the signs down the steps to the house. You can't see the house from the road, but it is below Victoria road between the road and the sea.

From Town

View Directions

1. Head southwest on Kloof Nek Rd/M62
2. At the top of the neck, turn right to go down Kloof Road
3. At the T-junction with Lower Kloof, turn left towards Camps Bay
4. Go past Camps Bay High School
5. At the stop street and T-junction on Victoria road (the sea should be directly in front of you) turn right towards Clifton
6. The house is on your left. If you're in Clifton, you've gone too far.

From Sea Point

View Directions

1. Head southwest on Beach Rd toward Camps Bay
2. At the roundabout, take the 1st exit onto Queens Rd
3. Turn right onto Victoria Rd
4. Continue along Victoria Road for 3 km, past Clifton, until you see the signs. The house is on your right.
5. If you've passed Strathmore Road and are in Camps Bay, you've gone too far.

From Hout Bay

View Directions

1. Head northeast on Victoria Road towards Camps Bay
2. From the robots at the intersection of Victoria Road and Geneva Drive, it is exactly 1 km to the house
3. The house is on your left, just past the turnoff to Lower Kloof Road
4. If you're in Clifton, you've gone too far.
